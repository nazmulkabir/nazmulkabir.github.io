Motivation

I recently gave a talk on writing reproducible manuscripts using R Markdown and the R package papaja (preparing apa journal articles). One of my main motivations for using R to write up my manuscripts is contained in a tweet by Pamela Jacobsen:

Why does so much of academia involve cutting and pasting information from one document to another document?

Pamela Jacobsen (May 17, 2021)

A reproducible workflow

In the workshop, I go through methods that keep you from manually transcribing and/or copy pasting tables, figures, statistical output, and citations over and over again.
