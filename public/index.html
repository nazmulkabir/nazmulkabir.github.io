About me

I am a Research Assistant Professor at Old Dominion University’s Commonwealth Cyber Initiative Coastal Virginia, where I explore emerging directions in cybersecurity. Previously, I was a Presidential Postdoctoral Fellow at Virginia Tech. My work spans AI assurance, machine learning for cyberattack detection, and securing cyber-physical systems, with deployments in critical infrastructure including water distribution and agricultural networks.
