Feras A. Batarseh leads research at the intersection of AI assurance and applied cyberbiosecurity, focusing on resilient water and agricultural systems and data-driven policy design. He directs the A3 Lab at Virginia Tech and collaborates across disciplines to translate AI methods into trustworthy, high-impact solutions.
